One hub. Zero brand inconsistencies.

Proactively designed and built an internal design hub to centralize brand assets, templates, and tools for cross-functional teams. The goal was to eliminate inconsistencies, reduce repetitive requests, and make brand execution frictionless across marketing, sales, and operations. Built entirely in Figma Make without a developer - the platform includes a live brand guideline system, QR code generator, email banner builder, headshot generator, and a centralized resource library.
